Melbourne Commute POV is an interactive Melbourne public transport map that estimates commute time from a chosen origin across the city, including train, tram, bus, walking, and V/Line connections.

Destination labels show the estimated travel time followed by mode chips, such as Walk, Train, Tram, Bus, or V/Line, summarising the services used by the fastest path.

Travel times are estimated from PTV GTFS trips (trains, trams, and frequent buses) plus short walking access to and from stops. V/Line regional trains are included; regional coaches, real-time schedules, and disruptions are not.
